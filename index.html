How it works

Three steps to your
neighborhood marketplace

From idea to first trade in minutes. No phone number, no complications.

01
📧

Sign up with email

No phone number required. Just your email address — or sign in with Apple or Google in one tap.

02
🏠

Claim your home

Enter the verification code from your NearNest postcard, or get vouched for by a verified neighbor on your street.

03
🤝

Start trading

Post items you have, request things you need. Chat in-app, arrange pickup, and meet your neighbors.

Why NearNest

Built different.
For your block.

Most marketplaces focus on users. NearNest focuses on homes.

🏠

Your home has an identity

Every house on your street can claim its own verified presence. Your address is your reputation — not just a delivery destination.

✓

Verified by address

Trust through physical verification. A postcard code or a neighbor's referral means listings come from real homes on your street.

🚫 No phone number required
💬

In-app chat only

No sharing phone numbers with strangers. All conversations stay in NearNest — safe, private, and tied to the listing.

🔍

Post requests too

Looking for a hibiscus cutting or spare eggs? Post a request and let neighbors come to you. Both supply and demand — on one street.

📍

Hyper-local, by design

NearNest is not a city-wide marketplace. It's your block, your street, your immediate neighbors. Browse items from half a mile to 5 miles — you choose the radius. Everything is meant to be picked up in person, on foot or a short drive away.
